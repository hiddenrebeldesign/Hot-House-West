Django In Orbit




"'Django In Orbit', a memorable and joyful listening experience!"

♦

"With lively performances of classic standards, Django obscurities, and a selection of originals, ‘Django In Orbit’ is a fresh take on Gypsy jazz and vintage, American swing. Colorful horn and violin solos, a solid rhythm section, Django-inspired lead guitar, and a diverse repertoire make ‘Django In Orbit’ a memorable and joyful listening experience. Hot House West proves that superb Gypsy jazz does not only come from France, Europe, or New York, and demonstrates that a modern Gypsy jazz group is not restricted to sounding like a duplicate of the Quintet of the Hot Club of France. They have their own unique musical personality that is always fun and enjoyable to hear.""

—Scott Yanow
Jazz Historian & Journalist
